# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Permi is built in Nigeria, for a global community. We are committed to
maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone
who participates in this project — regardless of age, background, nationality,
gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or level of experience.

We believe that a diverse community makes Permi stronger. The strongest
security tools are built by people who bring different perspectives,
different contexts, and different problems to solve.

---

## Our Standards

### Behaviour that strengthens our community

- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, not people. Critique code and
  proposals, not contributors.
- **Be constructive.** When giving feedback, explain what could be improved
  and why. Pair criticism with suggestions.
- **Be patient.** Not everyone has the same experience level or first
  language. A question that seems basic deserves a genuine answer.
- **Be honest.** If you do not know something, say so. If you made a
  mistake, own it. The community grows through honesty.
- **Be generous.** Share your knowledge. Help newcomers. Acknowledge
  good contributions publicly.
- **Be focused.** Keep discussions relevant to Permi and security tooling.

### Behaviour that weakens our community

The following are not acceptable in any Permi space — issues, pull
requests, discussions, social media, or any other venue:

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats of any kind
- Discriminatory language or jokes targeting any group
- Personal attacks or sustained criticism of an individual rather than
  their work
- Sharing private information about others without consent
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or repeated off-topic posts
- Dismissing or belittling contributions from less experienced community
  members
- Any behaviour that a reasonable person would consider hostile,
  harmful, or exclusionary

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- The Permi GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions, code reviews)
- Any official Permi communication channels
- Any public spaces where an individual is representing Permi or its community

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct,
please report it.

**Contact:** dashenasarahpeter@gmail.com

Reports are handled confidentially. You will receive an acknowledgement
within 48 hours and a response within 7 days describing what action,
if any, was taken.

When reporting, please include:
- A description of the incident
- Where it occurred (link to the issue, PR, or conversation)
- Any relevant screenshots or context
- Whether you want your name kept private in any follow-up

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behaviour they determine to be in violation of this Code
of Conduct.

Responses to violations may include:

| Severity | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Minor, first offence | Private written warning with explanation |
| Repeated minor or single significant offence | Temporary suspension from community spaces |
| Severe or repeated significant offences | Permanent ban from all Permi spaces |

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may face
consequences determined by the project lead.
